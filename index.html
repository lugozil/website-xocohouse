Discover Xoco House

Where rustic charm meets contemporary elegance.

Set in Chicago’s vibrant Pilsen neighborhood, Xoco House Gallery seamlessly blends artistic expression with elegant celebration. Our gallery-style space is designed with both beauty and functionality in mind.

A spacious open floor plan, soaring timber ceilings and an abundance of natural light give the venue a grand, contemporary character with a warm vintage soul — a refined atmosphere ready for whatever you imagine.
